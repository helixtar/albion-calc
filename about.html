About AlbionMarketCalc

A free, community-built trading toolkit for Albion Online players. No login required. No pay-walls. Just live market data and tools that help you make smarter silver.

🔧 Tools

📊
Market Browser
Available since launch
Browse 200+ items with live prices and item icons. Filter by category, tier and city. Click any item to open its full price page.
🔍
Flip Finder
Available since launch
Scans all city pairs in real time and ranks buy-low / sell-high opportunities by profit margin. Includes outlier filtering to remove stale listings.
⚖️
Market Calculator
Added · March 2026
Enter buy price, sell price, quantity and market tax for exact net profit and ROI. Tax presets: 3% Premium / 6% Standard / 8.5% City. Results scroll into view on Calculate.
🔥
Most Profitable Craft Today
Added · March 2026
Fetches live material costs vs sell prices for 60+ recipes and ranks by profit margin. Filter by city, tier and category. Live mini card also shown on the home page.

📡 Data Source

All market data comes from the Albion Online Data Project — a community-run open API that collects real-time market data from players running the data client.

API endpoints used

/api/v2/stats/prices/{ids}?locations={cities}&qualities=1

/api/v2/stats/history/{id}?locations={city}&time-scale=1

We query West EU primary with Europe as fallback, and corsproxy.io as a CORS fallback. Prices update as players scan markets in-game — freshness depends on community participation.
